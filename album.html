  • The Ferris-wheel insight
    December 30, 2018
    A girl once told me that she was searching for reasons to live. And she asked me why we push so hard if one day we're going to leave. I told her that life is like a Ferris-wheel. And no, it's not because one moment we're up there and another time down - is better than this.
    When I was a kid, I was scared of going on roller coasters, and I liked going on the Ferris-wheel. There were few - I think two - times that I had the wonderful opportunity to climb on one. I only went on a roller coaster once, and it was one of those where the ride was a green caterpillar, for kids. Was cool.
    But the Ferris-wheel is a totally different ride from the other attractions in the amusement park. It's not funny. But there's always one in the parks and there are huge queues in it. The point is that the fun of the Ferris-wheel is not in it. The secret is in who you take with you. It's not about the Ferris wheel. It's about the cabin.
    With the right one(s), even the queue is worth it.
    And this is one of the strongest insights I ever had. I take it with me since then.
    And let me tell you - the Ferris-wheels in my life gave me more butterflies in my stomach than the roller coasters.
